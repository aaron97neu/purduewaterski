Texas Water Ski

The University of Texas at Austin Collegiate Water Ski Team

About Us

We represent The University at sanctioned tournaments of the South Central Conference of the American Ski Association. We are a RecSports sponsored organization. The team has a boat and skis at our lake Frameswitch year round.

Join the team

No prior experience is required to join the team, just a desire to learn. The team provides all necessary equipment and instruction. We meet at 9PM every Monday in UTC 1.104, and are open to new members throughout the semester. If you have any questions don't hesitate to email us at utexaswaterski@gmail.com.
