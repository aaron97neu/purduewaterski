About Us

We are the official waterski team of the University of Texas at Austin! We are a division 2, competitive club team, and we represent The University of Texas at all sanctioned waterski tournaments in the South Central Conference of the American Ski Association. We are a RecSports sponsored organization. Visit our About page to learn more about our team and collegiate waterskiing!
